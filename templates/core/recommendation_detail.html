Optimization Insights
Tilt Angle Explanation

The optimal tilt angle ({{ recommendation.optimal_tilt }}°) is calculated based on your location's latitude to maximize solar exposure throughout the year. This angle captures the most solar energy on average.

Azimuth Angle Explanation

The optimal azimuth angle ({{ recommendation.optimal_azimuth }}°) points your panels toward the sun's path. For most locations in the Northern Hemisphere, this is typically south-facing (180°).

Expected Benefits

By adjusting to the optimal configuration, you can maximize your solar energy production and potentially reduce your electricity costs over time.
